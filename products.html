Products

Book Shop informs booksellers of new titles and helps handsell books to customers. Our exclusive IndieBound edition is packed with reviews, interviews and recommendations that will inspire your customers to read and buy more books. The bookstore edition features the IndieBound logo on the Front Cover masthead and the IndieNext List on the back cover. Our monthly print magazine is distributed to 400,000 avid readers through subscribing bookstores and public libraries across 48 states. Additionally, BookPage serves as a guide for subscribing booksellers who use our coverage to set new title buys and provide quality recommendations to their patrons.
